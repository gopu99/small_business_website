About

We baked with love

Since 1985. Richardsons has been creating cakes, desserts, chocolates. and savouiers delighting aficionados with every little bite. We are one of the organic and natural bakery in England. Guests come to bakery for unusual gourment desserts and incredible atmosphere of warmth and friendliness.

How we work

Serving a dessert is the real culmination of the evening. Children and adults are waiting impatiently for dessert. We bake the cake and prepare the cream as you want, using only natural ingredients of high quality. Putting the base cake. We are awaiting for the biscuits to infuse and soak. We think over the design, the colour scheme and every detail of the cake and proceed to the decor.
